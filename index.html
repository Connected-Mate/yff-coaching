Who Am I?

Yohan Plier

A coach driven by passion for fitness and well-being.

I've loved sports since I was young, and that passion led me to study and work in the field of physical performance and sports coaching.

I studied Sports Science (STAPS) with a specialization in Sports Training and Performance Optimization, then pursued a Bachelor's degree in Fitness Professions. I've worked for nearly four years as a coach in gyms and at home.

Now based in Toulouse, I'm here to help you reach your full potential.
